# CHAO AI Lab （面向开放式智能体智能的协同混合架构实验室）


### **About CHAO-AI Lab**
**CHAO-AI Lab** stands for **Collaborative Hybrid Architectures for Open-ended Agentic Intelligence Lab**, led by Prof. WangChao. We study how large language models, intelligent agents, retrieval-augmented systems, natural language processing, and time-series data analytics can be combined into **collaborative hybrid architectures** that support **open-ended agentic intelligence** in complex, real-world environments.

Our research is grounded in a simple belief: the next generation of AI systems will not come from a single model or a single algorithm, but from the careful design of **architectures** in which models, tools, data, knowledge, humans, and multiple agents collaborate. We aim to build AI systems that can understand language, retrieve and integrate knowledge, analyze heterogeneous data, reason over long horizons, interact with humans and other agents, and act reliably on open-ended tasks.


### **Research Vision**

We organize our research around three principles that together form the name **CHAO-AI**:

**Collaborative.** Intelligence emerges from collaboration—between humans and AI, between multiple agents, and between models, tools, and data systems. We study how to design interaction protocols, communication mechanisms, and coordination strategies that make such collaboration efficient, controllable, and trustworthy.

**Hybrid.** Real-world intelligence requires the integration of heterogeneous components: large language models, retrieval systems, structured knowledge, tools, code execution, time-series analytics, and traditional machine learning. We investigate how to combine these components into coherent architectures that exploit the strengths of each.

**Architectures for Open-ended Agentic Intelligence.** We focus on architectures—not isolated tricks—that enable agents to operate in **open-ended** settings, where tasks are under-specified, environments are dynamic, and information is incomplete. The goal is **agentic intelligence**: AI systems that can plan, retrieve, analyze, decide, and act, in service of complex human goals.



### **Research Areas**

**Large Language Models and Foundation Reasoning.** Reasoning, planning, long-context understanding, knowledge integration, and capability evaluation of LLMs in complex tasks.

**Agentic AI and Tool Use.** Architectures for LLM-based agents, including planning, memory, reflection, tool calling, code execution, browser interaction, and long-horizon task completion.

**Multi-Agent Systems and Interaction.** Communication, coordination, role specialization, debate, and cooperation among multiple agents, with applications to research, analysis, and decision support.

**Retrieval-Augmented and Knowledge-Enhanced Systems.** Information retrieval, retrieval-augmented generation, structured knowledge integration, and grounding agents in trustworthy information sources.

**Natural Language Processing.** Language understanding, information extraction, semantic representation, and language interfaces for agentic systems.

**Time-Series Mining and Data Analytics.** Modeling, mining, and analysis of temporal and multi-source data, with a focus on integrating analytical capabilities into agentic workflows.

**Human-AI Collaboration and Trustworthy Agents.** Human-in-the-loop design, controllable agents, alignment with user intent, and interpretable and reliable agent behavior in open-ended settings.



### **Core Questions We Care About**

How can large language models be turned into reliable, long-horizon agents that act safely in open-ended environments?

How should we design hybrid architectures that combine LLMs, retrieval, tools, knowledge, and analytics so that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts?

How can multiple agents communicate, coordinate, and specialize to solve problems that no single agent could solve alone?

How can humans and AI agents collaborate in research, analysis, and decision-making, while keeping humans informed and in control?

How can we build agents that genuinely understand temporal and analytical data, not just text, and turn raw data into trustworthy insights?



### **One-line Goal**

**CHAO-AI Lab — Collaborative Hybrid Architectures for Open-ended Agentic Intelligence.**

Building the next generation of AI systems where models, agents, tools, data, and humans work together.
